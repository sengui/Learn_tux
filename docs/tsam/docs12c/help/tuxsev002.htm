Managing Services

You can suspend or resume the selected services in the current server.

  1. From the Tuxedo Server action bar, click Services.

  2. In the Credentials page that appears, choose a Credential according to the screen prompts and click OK.

  3. In the Service page, do one of the following:

    • Click Suspend to prevent the selected services from providing service.

    • Click Resume to resume the selected services to provide service.
