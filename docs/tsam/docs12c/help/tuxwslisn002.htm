Viewing and Managing Handlers

You can view all the clients under a handler, suspend, resume, or kill the selected handlers.

  1. From the top action bar, click Handlers.

  2. In the Credentials page that appears, choose a Credential according to the screen prompts and click OK.

  3. In the Handlers page, do one of the following:

    • Click Killing to kill the selected handlers.

    • Click Client to show all the clients under the first selected handler.
