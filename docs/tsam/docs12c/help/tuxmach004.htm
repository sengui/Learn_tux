Managing Native Clients

You can suspend, resume or kill the selected clients under the current machine.

  1. From the Tuxedo Machine action bar, click Native Clients.

  2. In the Credentials page that appears, choose a Credential according to the screen prompts and click OK.

  3. In the Client page, do one of the following:

    • Click Suspend to prevent the selected clients from accessing Tuxedo system.

    • Click Resume to resume the suspended clients to access Tuxedo system.

    • Click Killing to kill the selected clients.
